#### Official Resources
- **[Daggerheart SRD](https://www.daggerheart.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/DH-SRD-May202025.pdf)** - The whole handbook, minus the art and a few little things. Good for character creation!
- **[The *Void*](https://www.daggerheart.com/thevoid/)** - Interested in the new, unreleased classes, abilities and spells to use with your character? Just check with me first!

#### Unofficial Resources
- [Daggerheart.org](https://daggerheart.org/) - The wikidot of Daggerheart. Go here for rules, character creation, and card references! (see below for direct links)

#### Direct Links For Character Creation

- **[Classes](https://daggerheart.org/reference/classes)** - The core of your character's abilities and specializations.
- **[Ancestries](https://daggerheart.org/reference/ancestries)** - Ancestries are your race/species. They give you two innate abilities.
- **[Communities](https://daggerheart.org/reference/communities)** - Communities give you one ability, and help you understand where you came from.
- **[Domain Cards](https://daggerheart.org/reference/all-domain-cards#)** - Use the top navigation bar to parse through domains, spells, abilities, or all!
- **[Weapons](https://daggerheart.org/reference/weapons) & [Armour](https://daggerheart.org/reference/armor)** - Tables for picking your main equipment.
